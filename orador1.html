Daniel Raposo

Daniel Raposo (PhD in Design) is communication designer and Reader / Principal Lecturer in Communication Design at the Polytechnic Institute of Castelo Branco, works, mainly, in corporate visual identity and Branding areas.

He lives in Castelo Branco where he teaches at the Polytechnic Institute of Castelo Branco; from 2006 to 2010 he was director of the degree in Communication Design and Audiovisual Production and was director of the Masters in Graphic Design ESART-IPCB in association with FAUTL until june 2014. Was responsible for the project "Rhythms of Weimar, " developed by ESART and Bauhaus-Universität Weimar.

Specialties: He is dedicated to design study since 1993, has a technical course in design by the Secondary School Nuno Alvares of Castelo Branco; a degree in Communication Design, Graphic Design and Advertising Variant (by ESTGP); a Master in Design, Materials and Product Management (by the University of Aveiro); A Doctorate in Design from the Faculdade de Arquitetura da Universidade de Lisboa (FAUTL), with a thesis titled "Letter as a sign of Corporate Visual Identity."
